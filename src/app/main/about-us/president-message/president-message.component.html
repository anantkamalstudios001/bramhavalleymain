Message from the President

Welcome to Brahma Valley University, a hub of learning and innovation in Nashik, Maharashtra. As President, I am dedicated to creating an environment that nurtures academic excellence, fosters inclusivity, and empowers students to become global leaders.

Biography

Dr. Rajesh Sharma has served as President of Brahma Valley University since 2020, bringing over 25 years of experience in higher education leadership. With a Ph.D. in Educational Administration from Mumbai University, Dr. Sharma has a proven track record of advancing academic programs and fostering community engagement.

Prior to joining Brahma Valley, he held senior leadership roles at prestigious institutions across India, where he spearheaded initiatives in curriculum development, research innovation, and diversity. His contributions have been recognized with the National Education Leadership Award in 2022.
