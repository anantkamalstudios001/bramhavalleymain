Our Committees

Brahma Valley University is committed to fostering a collaborative and well-governed academic environment. Our committees play a pivotal role in ensuring academic excellence, student welfare, and institutional growth. Each committee is composed of dedicated faculty, staff, and student representatives working together to uphold our mission and values.

Student Welfare Committee

The Student Welfare Committee is dedicated to enhancing student life by addressing their academic, social, and personal needs. It organizes extracurricular activities, provides counseling services, and ensures a supportive campus environment.

  • Student mentorship programs
  • Campus safety and well-being
  • Extracurricular event planning
Research and Innovation Committee

This committee fosters a culture of research and innovation by supporting faculty and student research projects, securing funding, and promoting industry collaborations to address real-world challenges.

  • Research grant facilitation
  • Innovation hubs and incubators
  • Industry-academia partnerships
Diversity and Inclusion Committee

The Diversity and Inclusion Committee promotes an inclusive campus culture, ensuring equal opportunities for all students and staff. It addresses issues of equity, diversity, and accessibility across the university.

  • Diversity training programs
  • Accessibility initiatives
  • Community outreach programs
