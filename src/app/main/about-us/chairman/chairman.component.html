Biography

Welcome to NGSPM’s Brahma Valley Institute Of Pharmacy Anjaneri, Nashik. We feel proud to welcome you. It is a matter of great pride that in the past fifteen years of its glorious journey, has made consistent progress year after year in the field of education. Teachers and students are the vital organs of any institution. Teachers are the ideal to their pupils.

Their activities and values leave an everlasting impression on the minds of students. I am sure during the course of Brahma Valley Institute Of Pharmacy ( BVIOP ). next four years, you will experience the quality-based value education. The College is emerging as a popular institution due to the excellent academic activities in the field of research, extension activities including Sports, Student Development and NSS. Gradually but steadily the College has grown in stature to become a center of excellence due to the untiring efforts of its illustrious faculty, dedicated supporting staff & the visionaries. I am pleased to extend my best wishes to all the members of the dedicated staff and students for all their future endeavours.
