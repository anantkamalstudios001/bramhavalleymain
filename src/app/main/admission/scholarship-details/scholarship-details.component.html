Scholarship Opportunities

Discover our range of scholarships designed to reward academic excellence, support financial need, and promote diversity.

Merit-Based Scholarships

Academic Excellence Scholarship

Eligibility: Students with 90% or above in their qualifying exams (12th grade or equivalent).

Benefits: Up to 50% tuition fee waiver for the first year.

Application: Submit academic transcripts and a statement of purpose during the admission process.

Entrance Exam Toppers Scholarship

Eligibility: Top 10 rank holders in university-approved entrance exams (e.g., JEE, CET).

Benefits: Full tuition fee waiver for the first semester.

Application: Provide entrance exam rank certificate along with the application form.

Need-Based Scholarships

Financial Aid Scholarship

Eligibility: Students from families with an annual income below INR 5,00,000.

Benefits: Up to 75% tuition fee waiver based on financial need assessment.

Application: Submit income certificate, bank statements, and a financial aid application form.

Rural Student Support Scholarship

Eligibility: Students from designated rural areas with demonstrated financial need.

Benefits: Covers 30% of tuition and hostel fees.

Application: Provide proof of residence and financial documents during application.

Special Scholarships

International Student Scholarship

Eligibility: Non-Indian students with a valid student visa and strong academic records.

Benefits: Up to 40% tuition fee waiver for the duration of the program.

Application: Submit passport, visa, and academic records with the application.

Sports Excellence Scholarship

Eligibility: Students with state or national-level achievements in sports.

Benefits: Up to 50% tuition fee waiver and sports facility access.

Application: Provide sports certificates and a recommendation from a coach.

How to Apply for Scholarships

Step 1: Complete Admission Application

Submit your admission application through the online portal before applying for scholarships.

Step 2: Gather Required Documents

Prepare academic transcripts, financial documents, certificates, or other required materials as per the scholarship criteria.

Step 3: Submit Scholarship Application

Complete the scholarship application form available on the university portal and upload all required documents by the deadline.
