Academic Rules

Attendance

Students must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in all courses to be eligible for examinations. Absences must be justified with prior approval or valid documentation.

Academic Integrity

Plagiarism, cheating, or any form of academic dishonesty is strictly prohibited and may result in disciplinary action, including suspension or expulsion.

Submission Deadlines

All assignments and projects must be submitted by the specified deadlines. Late submissions may incur penalties unless an extension is approved by the instructor.

Campus Rules

Code of Conduct

Students must respect fellow students, faculty, and staff. Any form of harassment, bullying, or disruptive behavior is not tolerated.

Campus Safety

Smoking, alcohol consumption, and use of prohibited substances are strictly forbidden on campus premises.

Property Respect

Damaging or defacing university property, including hostel facilities, will result in fines and disciplinary action.

Admission Rules

Application Submission

All applications must be submitted online by the specified deadline. Incomplete applications will not be processed.

Document Verification

Applicants must provide authentic documents for verification. Any falsification will lead to cancellation of admission.

Fee Payment

Admission fees must be paid by the due date to confirm enrollment. Non-payment may result in forfeiture of the seat.
